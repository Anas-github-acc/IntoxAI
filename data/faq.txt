xAI FAQ

1. What is xAI's mission?
xAI's mission is to accelerate human scientific discovery and advance our collective understanding of the universe.

2. What products does xAI build?
xAI develops artificial intelligence tools, including Grok, a conversational AI designed to provide helpful and truthful answers.

3. How does Grok work?
Grok uses advanced machine learning techniques to understand and respond to a wide range of questions, often with a unique outside perspective on humanity.
